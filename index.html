This is a github hosted page for AviAlibi, and all related net-published data.

Typo MC Races and Classes

Races

Races will be a means of selecting your species on the server. Your species will give you buffs and debuffs based on your location (biome, distance from the top and bottom of the world, the world type[normal, nether, end]) what time it is (always night in nether and end), this can effect your favor in certain towns (player developed)

Classes

Classes will be a way of improving your skills in a certain area.
The 3 class types are Combat, Gathering, and Traveling
